Origin of Akan Names

Akan names are derived from the day of the week on which a person is born. The Akan people believe that the day of birth influences a person's character and destiny.

Key Components:
  • Day Names: Specifically for males and females based on the seven-day week.
  • Ancestral Roots: Linked to the Asante and Fante people of Ghana and Ivory Coast.
